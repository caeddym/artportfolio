Work and Payment

The project will begin once all the necessary information and reference materials are provided by the client. Work will proceed according to the agreed timeline and is expected to conclude by the scheduled deadline. As the artist, I will create a custom illustration or design based on the client's request. Payment will be divided into two parts: 50% after the sketch has been approved, and the remaining 50% upon completion of the final product. Please note that the prices listed are base rates and may vary depending on the complexity or details of the project.
